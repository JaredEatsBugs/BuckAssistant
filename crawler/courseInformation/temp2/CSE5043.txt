CSE 5043 - Overview of Computer Systems For Non-Majors
Description:Introduction to computer architecture and organization at machine and assembly level; pointers and addressing using C programming; introduction to operating system concepts: process, memory management, file system and storage, and multi-threaded programming. Prereq: 5022 or equiv, and 5032 or equiv. Not open to students with credit for 2421, 5042, 2431, 3430, 360, or 660. Not open to CSE/CIS majors.
