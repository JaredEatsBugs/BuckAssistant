CSE 6421 - Computer Architecture
Description:Principles and tradeoffs behind the design of modern computer architectures, including instruction-level parallelism, memory system design, advanced cache architectures, cache coherence, multiprocessors, energy-efficient and embedded architectures. Prereq: 3431 (660) or 5431, and 3421 (675), 5421, or ECE 5362 (662). Not open to students with credit for 775.
