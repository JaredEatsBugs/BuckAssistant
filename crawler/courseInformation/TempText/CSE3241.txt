CSE 3241 - Introduction to Database Systems
Description
Database systems use, logical design, entity-relationship model, normalization, query languages and SQL, relational algebra and calculus, object relational databases, XML, active databases; database design project.Prereq: 2133 or 2231, and 2321. Not open to students with credit for 5241.
