CSE 5914 - Capstone Design: Knowledge-Based Systems
Description
Capstone design project; conceptual and technical design; theory and practice of knowledge-based systems; teamwork, written and oral communication skills.Prereq: 2501 (601) or 5501, and 3901 (560) or 3902 or 3903, and 3521 (630) or 5521. Not open to students with credit for 731. Class Notes: If you are trying to get on the waitlist, but can't (because the pre-reqs are enforced), contact Kitty Reeves.92.
