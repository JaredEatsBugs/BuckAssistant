CSE 5042 - Systems I: Introduction to Low-Level Programming and Computer Organization
Description
Introduction to computer architecture at machine and assembly language level; pointers and addressing; C programming at machine level; computer organization. Prereq: 2122, 2123, 2231 or 321; and 2321, Math 2566, or 366. Not open to students with credit for 2421 or 360.
