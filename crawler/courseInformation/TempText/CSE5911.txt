CSE 5911 - Capstone Design: Software Applications
Description
Capstone design project: application of software engineering techniques, methodologies and technologies in software lifecycle activities using enterprise software frameworks; teamwork, written and oral communication.Prereq: 2501 (601) or 5501, and 3901 (560) or 3902 or 3903, and 3231 (757) or 5231. Not open to students with credit for 758 or 762.
