CSE 1110 - Introduction to Computing Technology
Description
A course of general interest giving experience with personal computer software, e.g., word processors and spreadsheets; provides fundamental computer literacy; neither teaches nor requires programming. Prereq: Not open to students with credit for 1111 (101), 1113, or 100. Class Notes: Please note that although the delivery method of this course is distance learning, there are 3 different points during the regular term where attendance on main campus is mandatory: one component of lab 1 must be completed in Caldwell Labs, and the midterm exam and the final exam must be taken onsite. Location to be announced in course syllabus.
