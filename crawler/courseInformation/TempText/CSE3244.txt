CSE 3244 - Data Management in the Cloud
Description
Systematic organization of data on cloud computing architectures; basic indexing techniques, including B-tree and hash-based indexing; fundamentals of query optimization, including access path selection and cardinality estimation; full and partial replication; data partitioning and distributed task scheduling.Prereq: 3241 or 5241; and 2421 or 3430.
