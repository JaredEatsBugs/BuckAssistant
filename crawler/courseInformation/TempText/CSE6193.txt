CSE 6193 - Individual Studies in Computer Science and Engineering
Description
Designed to give the student an opportunity to pursue special studies not otherwise offered. Prereq: Permission of instructor. Repeatable to a maximum of 10 cr hrs or 10 completions. This course is graded S/U. Class Notes: Please be sure you have permission from the instructor to sign up for this course as well as for the number of credit hours.
