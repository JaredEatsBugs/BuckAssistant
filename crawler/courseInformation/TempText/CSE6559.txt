CSE 6559 - Advanced Studies in Computer Graphics
Description
Advanced-level topics in computer graphics. Repeatable to a maximum of 24 cr hrs or 8 completions. This course is graded S/U. Class Notes: Title: Scientific Data Management, Analysis, and VisualizationDescription: In this class, we will discuss advanced topics in scientific data management, analysis, and visualization. Selected papers from top conferences in the related areas will be studied. Students are required to present 1-2 papers and also activity participate in the discussion of the papers.
