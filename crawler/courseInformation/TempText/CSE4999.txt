CSE 4999 - Computer Science and Engineering Research for Thesis
Description
Supervised research and project work arranged individually. Prereq: Permission of instructor. Repeatable to a maximum of 10 cr hrs or 10 completions. This course is graded S/U. Class Notes: Please be sure you have permission from the instructor to sign up for this course as well as for the number of credit hours.
