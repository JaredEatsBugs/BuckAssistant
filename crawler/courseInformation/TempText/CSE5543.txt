CSE 5543 - Geometric Modeling
Description
Common algorithmic and mathematical techniques for modeling geometric objects in computer graphics and CAD applications; sample based modeling, mesh generation, and hierarchical representations.Prereq: Math 2568 (568) or 571. Not open to students with credit for 784.
