CSE 5052 - Survey of Artificial Intelligence for Non-Majors
Description
Survey of the basic concepts and techniques in artificial intelligence, including problem solving, knowledge representation, and machine learning. Prereq: 1211, 1221, 1222, 1223, or 2221, or Grad standing. Not open to students with credit for 3521 (630), 4521, or 5521, or students enrolled in a CSE or CIS major.
