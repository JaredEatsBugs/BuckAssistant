CSE 5915 - Capstone Design: Information Systems
Description
Capstone design project; information system principles: database design methods and tools, indexing, searching, application development, testing, evaluation; teamwork, written and oral communication skills.Prereq: 2501 (601) or 5501, and 3901 (560) or 3902 or 3903, and 3241 (670) or 5241. Not open to students with credit for 772. Class Notes: If you are trying to get on the waitlist, but can't (because the pre-reqs are enforced), contact Kitty Reeves.92.
